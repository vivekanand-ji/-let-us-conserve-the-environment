Causes of Pollution

Industrial Activities

Factories release smoke, chemicals, and waste into air and water, polluting both urban and rural areas.

Vehicle Emissions

Cars, buses, trucks, and airplanes produce exhaust gases like CO2, NOx, and particulate matter.

Deforestation

Cutting down trees increases air pollution, reduces oxygen levels, and contributes to climate change.

Improper Waste Disposal

Garbage, plastics, and hazardous materials contaminate soil and water bodies.

Agricultural Activities

Excessive use of pesticides, fertilizers, and chemical sprays contaminates soil and groundwater.

Urbanization & Construction

Dust, debris, and construction waste increase air and land pollution, affecting health and environment.
