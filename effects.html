Effects of Pollution on Earth

Global Warming

Excessive greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere, leading to rising temperatures worldwide.

Air Pollution

Smoke and chemicals in the air cause respiratory diseases, acid rain, and reduce air quality.

Water Pollution

Rivers, lakes, and oceans get contaminated with chemicals, waste, and plastics harming aquatic life.

Soil Degradation

Chemical waste, pesticides, and industrial dumping reduce soil fertility and harm plants.

Climate Change

Pollution affects weather patterns, causing floods, droughts, storms, and irregular rainfall.

Health Issues

Exposure to polluted air, water, and soil leads to respiratory, cardiovascular, and neurological problems.
