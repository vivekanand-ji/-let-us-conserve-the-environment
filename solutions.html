Solutions

Here are some practical solutions to protect the environment:

Action Example Impact
5R's Reduce,Reuse,Recycle,Refuse and Repurpose the items Decreases pollution and conserves resources
Rot(Compost) Compost kitchen wastes like vegetable peels Reduces organic waste in landfill,enriches soil naturally
Save water Fix leaking taps and use water properly Prevents scarcity and protects aquatic life
Plant Trees Community tree plantation drives Improves air quality and biodiversity
Eco-friendly Transport Walk, cycle, or use public transport Reduces vehicle emissions
